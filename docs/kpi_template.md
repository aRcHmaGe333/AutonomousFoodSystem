KPI Template — Minimal

Primary KPIs (daily/weekly):
- net_edible_yield_pct: delivered / harvested * 100
- food_waste_kg: total kg wasted per day
- time_to_consumer_median_hours: median hours from harvest to consumer
- fill_rate_pct: percent of requests satisfied from fresh inventory
- person_hours_saved_per_week: estimated manual hours avoided

Secondary KPIs:
- energy_kwh_per_kg
- water_l_per_kg
- mttr_hours
- uptime_pct
- equity_score (0-1)

Notes: store raw events and compute daily aggregates; use rolling 7?day windows for stability.
