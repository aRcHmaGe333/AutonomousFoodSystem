# Outreach Message Templates

## Purpose

These templates are for sending the dairy-first review packet to named external reviewers.

Keep the tone short, direct, and technical.
Do not ask for generic feedback.
Do not ask for endorsement.

## Standard Packet To Attach

Send these files together unless the contact format forces a link instead:

- SYSTEM_SUMMARY.md
- PROOF_STATUS_SUMMARY.md
- ARCHITECTURE_SUMMARY.md
- DAIRY_SUBSYSTEM_BRIEF.md
- ASSUMPTIONS_SHEET.md
- QUESTION_SHEET.md
- REVIEW_RESPONSE_TEMPLATE.md

## Private Email Judgment Rule

For private email, limited internal context is acceptable if it improves judgment speed.

Use that allowance narrowly:

- say what stage the work is in only when it helps the reviewer calibrate how hard to critique it
- say what decision you are trying to make if that sharpens the review ask
- say what you are uncertain about if that helps the reviewer target the weak point faster

Do not use private-email allowance as a license for internal process talk, repo talk, or long backstory.

Good private-email context:

- I am trying to determine whether this direction survives serious technical scrutiny before any stronger prototype or public claim
- I am deliberately looking for failure points, missing constraints, and measurements that would change the judgment

Still avoid:

- internal task framing
- workspace or repository references
- discussion of document plumbing
- long origin story or strategic vision language

## Short Email Template

Subject:

Request for technical review of dairy-first milking concept assumptions

Body:

Hello [Name],

I am looking for a short, technically decisive review of a dairy-first concept built around corridor geometry, passive or semi-passive positioning, explicit sanitation flow, and integrated waste-loop handling.

This is not a request for general innovation feedback or endorsement.
I want to know where the concept breaks, which assumptions are under-specified, and what measurements would be required before stronger claims are justified.

I have attached a self-contained review packet with:

- system summary
- proof-status summary
- architecture summary
- subsystem brief
- assumptions sheet
- question sheet
- response template

The useful outcome is a short response under these headings:

1. Credible as stated
2. Credible only with major caveats
3. Not credible or not yet supportable
4. Missing information needed for judgment
5. Recommended pilot measurements
6. Public claims that should be avoided for now

If written notes are inconvenient, I can also do a short call and capture the same structure afterward.

If this is outside your lane, a referral to someone more directly relevant would still be useful.

Regards,

[Name]

## Discipline Emphasis Hints

Adjust one paragraph depending on the reviewer:

- dairy engineering: emphasize corridor tolerances, cradle mechanics, attachment realism, and maintenance burden
- welfare: emphasize refusal risk, stress triggers, training dependence, and measurable welfare thresholds
- biosecurity: emphasize contamination paths, sanitation chemistry, verification, and zone separation realism
- digestion and waste: emphasize mass balance, pathogen reduction, digestate usefulness, and resource-loop constraints
- economics: emphasize intervention rate, staffing, maintenance hours, throughput, and opex per liter

## Do Not Say

- do not say the system is validated
- do not imply the reviewer is being asked to bless the whole vision
- do not send the full repo unless explicitly requested
- do not oversell prior synthetic or generic reports as completed expert review
