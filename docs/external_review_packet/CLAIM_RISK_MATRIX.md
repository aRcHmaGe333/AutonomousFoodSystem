## Purpose

This matrix maps concept claims to the reviewer disciplines most likely to validate, qualify, or reject them.

## Internal Use

- if a claim is rejected by its main discipline, remove or weaken the public wording
- if a claim survives only with caveats, keep the claim but carry the caveat into public materials
- if a claim cannot be judged yet, convert that into a measurement requirement rather than optimism
