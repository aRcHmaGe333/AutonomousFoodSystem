# Proof-Status Summary

## Reading rule

This summary separates four categories that should not be blended together:

- physically implemented
- simulated or modeled
- conceptual
- externally unvalidated

## Current status by area

### Controlled crop production

- simulated and software-supported
- not presented here as the main external review target

### Cooking and portioning

- partially automated in concept and software framing
- not presented here as the main external review target

### Dairy-first livestock concept

- partially modeled
- conceptually structured
- externally unvalidated

### Corridor and passive positioning logic

- conceptually defined
- not physically validated

### Cradle-based milking interaction

- conceptually defined
- not physically validated

### Sanitation flow

- explicitly recognized as necessary
- not physically validated

### Waste, digestion, and nutrient-loop assumptions

- directionally modeled
- not physically validated

### Welfare claims

- should be treated as hypotheses or design intentions
- not validated outcomes

### Operational economics

- should be treated as open questions
- not validated business conclusions

## What this means

The concept may be worthy of continued work.
It is not yet entitled to strong real-world claims.

Any external statement stronger than that would need independent evidence.
